# What We Carry

A quiet check-in for couples about the work of running a life — not just who does it
physically, but who holds it mentally.

→ **Try it:** https://what-we-carry.pages.dev/

## What it is

- A single HTML page. Open it, answer questions about everyday household work
  (180+ across all categories, though you can hide any category that doesn't apply),
  and see a shared picture of what each of you carries.
- **All answers stay on your device.** No server, no account, no analytics.
  You can unplug your internet and it still works.
- Designed around the "physical vs mental" distinction from Eve Rodsky's *Fair Play* —
  but this is an independent tool, not affiliated with or endorsed by her or the Fair Play brand.

## What it isn't

- Not a score, grade, or verdict on your relationship.
- Not therapy, and not a replacement for talking to each other — it's a conversation starter.
- Not a product. No paywall, no upsell, no newsletter.

## Use it

- **Hosted:** https://what-we-carry.pages.dev/
- **Self-host:** download `index.html` and open it in any browser. That's it.
